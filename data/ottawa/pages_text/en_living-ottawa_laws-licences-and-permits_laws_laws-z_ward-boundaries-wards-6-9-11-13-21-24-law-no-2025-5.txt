A by-law of the City of Ottawa to amend By-law No. 2021-3, as amended by the Ontario Land Tribunal, respecting the ward boundaries of Wards 6, 9, 11, 13, 21, 24.
The Council of the City of Ottawa enacts as follows:
1. Schedule A-2, Schedule A-8, Schedule A-11, Schedule A-13, Schedule A-16, and Schedule A-22 of By-law No. 2021-3 entitled “A by-law of the City of Ottawa to establish ward boundaries and Council composition” are amended to reflect the redistribution of lands as described in Schedules 1 to 3 attached hereto.
1. The ward boundary amendments described in Schedules 1 to 3 attached hereto are established for the City of Ottawa effective November 15, 2026;
1. This By-law includes Schedules 1 to 3 annexed hereto and they are hereby declared to form part of this by-law.

ENACTED AND PASSED this 22
nd
day of January 2025.

Planning application submission information and materials
Amending By-law 2024-444
The Council of the City of Ottawa enacts as follows:
In addition to the prescribed information identified by regulation made under the
Planning Act,
any person or public body that applies for:
an Official Plan amendment,
a Zoning By-law amendment,
a draft Plan of Subdivision approval,
Condominium approval,
Site Plan Control application
Urban and Village Boundary Expansion Official Plan amendment.
may be required to provide such other information that the City may need either at the time of filing an application or subsequently to assist in resolving any concerns identified through the review of the application.
The other information or materials that may be required to be provided are:
Application Form
Agrology and Soil Capability Study
Archaeological Assessment
Building Elevations
Community Energy Plan
Environmental Impact Study
Energy Modelling Report
Environmental Management Plan
Environmental Site Assessment (Phase 1 & Phase 2)
Geotechnical Study
Grading and Drainage Plan
Heritage Impact Assessment
Heritage Act Acknowledgment Report
Hydrogeological and Terrain Analysis
High-performance Development Standard
Impact Assessment Study - Mineral Aggregate
Impact Assessment Study - Mining Hazards
Impact Assessment Study - Waste Disposal Sites
I
Former Landfill Sites
Landscape Plan
Mature Neighbourhood Streetscape Character Analysis
Minimum Distance Separation
Noise Control Study
Parking Plan
Plan of Survey
Plan of Subdivision
Plan of Condominium
Planning Rationale
Preliminary Construction Management Plan
Public Consultation Strategy
Rail Proximity Study
Shadow Analysis
Site Plan
Site Servicing Study
Slope Stability Study
Transportation Impact Assessment
Tree Conservation Report
Urban Design Brief Urban Design Review Panel Report (mm)
Urban and Village Boundary Expansion - Land Needs Assessment
Urban and Village Area Boundary Expansion -  Settlement Area Parcel Analysis
Urban and Village Area Boundary Expansion - Infrastructure Capacity Assessment
Water Budget Assessment
Wellhead Protection Study
Wind Analysis
Zoning Confirmation Report
This by-law may be cited as the Information and Materials for Planning Applications By-law or the Development Application Study Policy as per Section 11.8(2) of the Official Plan.
By-law 2022-254 is repealed.
This by-law shall be deemed to come into force on July 1, 2023
